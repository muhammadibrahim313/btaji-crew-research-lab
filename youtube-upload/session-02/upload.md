## Title

Research Session 2: Gap, Literature Review, Research Question + Practical Project (Urdu/Hindi)

## Description

How do you find your own research? In this session we walk the full path from a simple idea to a clear research question, then show it live on two real projects.

Part A is the method: idea, hypothesis, literature review, analyzing papers, finding the gap, and writing the research question. Ibrahim walks through it using his own paper on tokenizer cost across languages. Part B is a full practical walkthrough, where Zulqarnain takes his final year project on rare cancer diagnosis and shows exactly how he built the literature review, proved every claim with a cited paper, found the gap, and reached his research questions.

Hosted by Muhammad Ibrahim Qasmi (Youngest 3x Kaggle Grandmaster) and Zulqarnain Ali (Kaggle Competition Expert). In Urdu with English slides.
