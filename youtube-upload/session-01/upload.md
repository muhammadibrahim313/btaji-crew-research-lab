## Title

Research Session 1: What Research Is & How to Read a Paper (Urdu/Hindi)

## Description

New to research? This first session takes you from zero to the two things every researcher needs: understanding what research actually is, and being able to read a paper without drowning in it.

We cover what makes something research: a clear claim, real evidence, and something new. We also cover how to think like a researcher instead of a user, the main types of research, and why research is slow but worth it. Then the main part: how to read a paper, shown live on two famous papers, Attention Is All You Need and ResNet.

Hosted by Muhammad Ibrahim Qasmi (Youngest 3x Kaggle Grandmaster) and Zulqarnain Ali (Kaggle Competition Expert). In Urdu with English slides.
